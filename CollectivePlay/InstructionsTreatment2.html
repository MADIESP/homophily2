Part 3 - Instructions - Collective Game


Based on your ranking, you will be paired with a partner for 6 rounds.

In every round:

Both of you will see the same table filled with 0s and 1s.

You will have 30 seconds to count all the 0s.

One partner's answer will be randomly chosen as the team's response.

If this answer is correct, both earn 1 point; if not, both earn 0 points.

Afterward, both receive feedback on the team's performance and may exchange a message with their partner.

If you have any question, please raise your hand, and the experimenter will assist you.

We will now walk you through an example to showcase the feedback types and possible messages to send.

Click the button when you are ready to start the example.
