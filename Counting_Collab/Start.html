Instructions


This is a group counting game with 8 rounds.

In each round, you'll see a table divided in half, filled with 0s and 1s, just like the previous part.

First, you have 45 seconds to individually count all the 0s in the table.

Next, you can chat with your partner to agree on the total numer of 0s to submit as the group's answer.

If the submitted total is correct, both you and your partner earn 2 points, and your team proceeds to the next table.

If the total is incorrect, you can chat again to submit a new answer. You both earn 0.5 points if the revised total is correct.

You have 12 minutes to complete this part. A message will alert you when there are only 2 minutes left.

Press the button when you are ready to start.
