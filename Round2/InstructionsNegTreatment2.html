Part 3 - Instructions - Collective Game


In this example, you are paired with Mary.

Mary and you have just counted the number of 0s in table 1.

One of your answers was randomly chosen as the team's answer, and this answer is wrong.

The identity of the partner whose answer was selected will be revealed.

The partner whose answer was not selected will select a message from a list and will send it to his/her partner.

On the left below is the screen you will see if your answer was chosen. In that case, Mary will send you a message.

On the right is the screen you will see if Mary's answer was chosen. In that case, you will select and send a message to Mary.



If you have any questions from this point on, please raise your hand and wait for the experimenter to come to you.

Press the button when you are ready to start the game.
