Part 3 - Instructions - Partner Selection


We will use your ranking to pair you with another player. It is important that you rank the CVs by order of preference.

The matching algorithm will work as follows:

One participant today will be randomly selected first. This participant will be paired to the player he/she ranked first.

A second participant will then be randomly selected. This participant will be paired to his/her most preferred player, provided that this player has not already been matched. If that player is already part of a team, he/she will be paired to his/her second most preferred player.

This process will be repeated until each player is paired.

Therefore, the higher up a person is in your ranking, the more likely you will be matched with that person.

If you have any questions from this point on, please raise your hand and wait for the experimenter to come to you.
