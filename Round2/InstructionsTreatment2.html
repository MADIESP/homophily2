Part 3 - Instructions - Collective Game


According to the matching algorithm described previously, you will be paired with a partner for 6 rounds.

In each round, both partners will see the same table, filled with 0s and 1s.

Each partner will have 30 seconds per round to count all the 0s.

One of the partners' answer will be randomly chosen as the team's answer.

If the team's answer is correct, both partners will earn 1 point. If the team's answer is incorrect, both partners will earn 0 points.

Ather each table, each partner will receive a feedback on the team's answer and might send a message to his/her partner.

If you have any questions from this point on, please raise your hand and wait for the experimenter to come to you.

To give you an idea of how the experiment will proceed, we will go through an example and show you the different types of feedback you will receive; and messages you will send.

Press the button when you are ready to start the example.
