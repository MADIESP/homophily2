Part 3 - Instructions - Partner Selection


Your ranking will determine your partner in the following manner:

1- A participant will be randomly selected.

2- This participant will be paired with their top-ranked partner.

3- If that partner is already paired, the participant will be paired with their next preferred choice.

4- Another participant will then be randomly selected. This participant will be paired with their top-ranked partner. If that partner is already paired, the participant will be paired with their next preferred choice.

5- This process continues until everyone is paired.

Therefore, the higher you rank a participant, the more likely you are to be matched with that participant.

If you have any question, please raise your hand, and the experimenter will assist you.

Click the button when you are ready to read the rest of the instructions.
